Phase Definitions
A&D — Analysis, Design & Discovery
Requirements gathering, technical deep dives, data assessment, solution architecture, and stakeholder alignment. Ends with confirmed SOW scope.
Build — Development, Testing & Validation
Iterative model development, feature engineering, labelling, training, test harness creation, and business validation cycles.
Benchmarking — Performance Evaluation
Accuracy assessment, business validation against agreed success metrics, load/latency testing, and stakeholder sign-off on model performance.
Prod Handover — Production Transition
Knowledge transfer, deployment artifacts, operational runbooks, monitoring setup, and CBA team go-live readiness confirmation.
Post Maintenance
Model monitoring, drift detection, re-training cycles, performance reports, and ongoing advisory support under the 20% maintenance fee structure.
